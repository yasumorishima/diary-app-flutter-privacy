# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 9, 2026**

Daily Diary (hereinafter referred to as "the App") respects the privacy of our users and is committed to protecting personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects and how it is used.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
The App stores the following information locally on your device:
- Diary content (text)
- Diary creation date and time
- Mood records
- Tag information

**This information is stored only on your device and is NOT sent to external servers.**

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information
The App may collect the following information for advertising purposes:
- Advertising ID (Android: AAID, iOS: IDFA)
- Device information (OS type, version, etc.)
- App usage data (ad impressions, etc.)

This information is collected by Google AdMob and used to optimize ad delivery.

## 2. How We Use Information

The collected information is used for the following purposes:
- Providing diary functionality (local storage only)
- Displaying in-app advertisements
- Measuring advertising effectiveness
- Improving the App

## 3. Data Storage

- **Diary Data**: Stored only in local storage on your device. The developer and third parties cannot access this data.
- **Advertising Data**: May be stored on Google AdMob servers.

## 4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

### Google AdMob
We use Google AdMob for ad delivery. AdMob may collect device information and advertising IDs to optimize ad delivery.

AdMob Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Opting Out of Personalized Ads
If you do not wish to receive personalized ads based on your advertising ID, you can disable it in your device settings:
- **Android**: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
- **iOS**: Settings > Privacy > Tracking > Allow Apps to Request to Track (turn off)

## 5. Data Deletion

### When You Uninstall the App
Uninstalling the App will delete all diary data stored on your device.

### Data Export
The App includes a feature to export diary data. If you wish to backup your data, please export it before uninstalling.

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 7. Security

The App takes reasonable measures to protect user information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or data storage is 100% secure.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as necessary. When changes are made, we will post the updated version on this page and update the "Last Updated" date.

## 9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please send us a review or feedback through the Google Play Store app page.
